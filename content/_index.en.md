**HardenLab Local Pack**

Windows hardening without Active Directory.

HardenLab Local Pack is a conservative, locally deployable hardening package for **Windows 11** in **standalone, workgroup and other small local environments without Active Directory**.

## What it is for

- small businesses without an in-house IT team
- locally administered standalone devices
- small environments without AD, Intune or MDM
- teams that want a supportable hardening baseline instead of a complex management stack

## What it is not for

- Active Directory / domain GPO
- Intune / MDM / Autopilot
- servers, RDS, domain controllers
- Office, Adobe or broader fleet hardening outside the documented scope
