## What this documentation covers

- target audience and scope of v1
- **Windows 11 Pro 25H2** as the primary target release and **24H2** as still supported
- supported and unsupported deployment scenarios
- principles for pilot device, backup and recovery
- rollout preparation in small workgroup environments
- legal and operational boundaries at a high level

## What this documentation deliberately does not replace

- full license documents
- full privacy notice
- individual project planning
- architecture or compliance consulting
- technical detail documentation for special environments outside the scope

## Rollout principle

The product is designed for **careful local deployment**:

1. verify the scope
2. prepare the target system properly
3. test on a pilot device first
4. review results and side effects
5. only then roll out further in a controlled way
