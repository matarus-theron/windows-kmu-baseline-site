# About HardenLab

HardenLab develops focused hardening products for small Windows environments — without overengineering and without enterprise prerequisites.

## Why HardenLab

Many security recommendations assume Active Directory, Intune, or an internal IT department. Small businesses often do not have that — and they are still being targeted.

HardenLab builds products for exactly this gap: locally deployable, clearly documented, and limited to a scope that fits small environments.

## What HardenLab stands for

- Clear scope instead of overpromising
- A finished product instead of a consulting project
- Technical honesty over marketing
- Conservative baselines that remain supportable
