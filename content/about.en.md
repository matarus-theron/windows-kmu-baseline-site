HardenLab develops focused hardening products for small Windows environments — without overengineering, without enterprise prerequisites.

## Why HardenLab

Many security recommendations assume Active Directory, Intune or an internal IT department. Small businesses don't have that — and they're still being attacked.

HardenLab builds products for exactly this gap: locally deployable, clearly documented and with a scope that fits small environments.

## What we stand for

- Clear scope instead of overpromising
- Finished product instead of consulting project
- Technical honesty over marketing
- Conservative baselines that remain supportable

## Made in Switzerland

HardenLab is developed in Switzerland, with a focus on SMBs in the German-speaking region.
