## Planned delivery scope

The intention is a clearly scoped product package for local deployment in small Windows 11 environments without Active Directory. The concrete purchase and delivery flow will be added later on this page. :contentReference[oaicite:16]{index=16}

## What buyers should expect

- clearly defined v1 scope
- documented product boundaries
- technical accompanying documentation
- focus on local applicability
- conservative rollout guidance with pilot device first :contentReference[oaicite:17]{index=17}

## Check before purchase

- does the target system fit the support matrix?
- is the target scenario really a workgroup / small setup?
- have the checklist and FAQ been reviewed?
- is a pilot device planned?
- are backup / snapshot / recovery prepared? :contentReference[oaicite:18]{index=18}

## Not yet included

- public checkout
- final pricing
- automatic download flow
- enabled purchase button
