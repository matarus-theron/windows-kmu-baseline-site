# Windows 11 Hardening for SMBs

## Variants

- **Solo** — up to 3 devices
- **Team** — up to 10 devices
- **Business** — up to 25 devices

## Scope before purchase

This product is intended for **small local Windows environments without Active Directory**.

Please do **not** buy it for:
- domain environments
- Intune / MDM-managed devices
- servers or RDS
- production-critical special environments without a pilot test

## Delivery model

- local deployment
- conservative, supportable scope
- controlled digital delivery
- no centrally managed fleet platform in v1
